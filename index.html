J's Production Ltd — Family Medicine Tracker

Our app helps families manage daily medications with ease. Track doses, set reminders, monitor stock levels, and share schedules with caregivers — all in one simple, calming interface.

Key Features

  • Daily medicine schedule with morning, afternoon, and evening grouping
  • Mark doses as taken, skipped, or missed with a quick tap
  • Stock tracking with automatic refill predictions
  • Family sharing so caregivers can stay in the loop
  • Large mode for accessibility with big buttons and high-contrast text
  • Emergency medical card for showing to medical staff
