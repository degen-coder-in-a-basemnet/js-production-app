Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

J's Production Ltd ("we", "our", "us") operates the Family Medicine Tracker mobile application. This page explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

When you use our app, we may collect:

  • Account info: Name and email address when you sign in via Google or Apple.
  • Medicine data: Medicine names, doses, and schedules you enter into the app.
  • Usage data: Dose tracking history (taken, skipped, missed).

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve the medicine tracking service.
  • To enable family sharing features between caregivers and patients.
  • To send dose reminders you have opted into.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase cloud infrastructure. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Your Rights

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.
